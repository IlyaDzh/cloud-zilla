Cloudzilla Network is a Decentralized Cold Data Storage, which is an aggregator of Decentralized Cloud Storages. They are best suited for the “cold” data, which is rarely used or accessed, but must be stored for a long time or even forever.

With our AI-based purchasing system, we act as a Uber for Clouds by providing our users a single entry point for any available Decentralized Cloud Storage, always at the best price.

Cloudzilla is a blockchain-based ecosystem and has its own ERC20 token called CLZ for payment purposes
